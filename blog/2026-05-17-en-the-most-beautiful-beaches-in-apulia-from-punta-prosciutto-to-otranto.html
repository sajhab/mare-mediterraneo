The Most Beautiful Beaches in Apulia: From Punta Prosciutto to Otranto

Explore the sun-kissed beaches of Apulia, from the pristine sands of Punta Prosciutto to the historical allure of Otranto. Discover local secrets for your Salento adventure.

Mare Mediterraneo — Torre Chianca, Salento · 400m from the sea · 15 min from Lecce

Introduction to Apulian Beaches

Nestled along the southeastern coast of Italy, the region of Apulia, known as Puglia in Italian, is a treasure trove of natural beauty and seaside delight. Stretching from the Adriatic Sea to the Ionian Sea, Apulia offers some of the most stunning beaches in Italy. For those staying at Mare Mediterraneo, located just 400 meters from the enchanting Torre Chianca, these beaches are a must-visit.

Punta Prosciutto: A Slice of Paradise

Known for its crystal-clear waters and soft, white sand, Punta Prosciutto is often likened to the Caribbean. This astonishing beach is a mere 25-minute drive from Torre Chianca, making it a convenient day trip for those based at Mare Mediterraneo. Punta Prosciutto is ideal for families and anyone looking to unwind in a serene setting. Bring your own beach umbrella and picnic, as the beach retains its untamed charm with minimal commercial development.

Embrace History and Beauty in Otranto

Traveling 35 minutes from Torre Chianca, you'll find Otranto, a town where history meets spectacular sea views. The Otranto Strand, set against the backdrop of a charming old town, offers sunbathing opportunities alongside cultural exploration. After a relaxing day on the beach, stroll through the ancient streets and visit the impressive Otranto Cathedral. For those who appreciate both beach and history, Otranto presents a perfect blend.

The Gem of Porto Cesareo

A 30-minute drive from Torre Chianca brings you to Porto Cesareo, where marine beauty is protected within a nature reserve. Known for its vibrant marine life, Porto Cesareo offers both gorgeous beaches and snorkeling opportunities. Enjoy fresh seafood at the local trattorias after a long swim in the azure waters. It's a delightful spot to enjoy what the Salento Meer deutsch is renowned for: turquoise water and a tranquil atmosphere.

Discover Gallipoli's Allure

At 45 minutes from Torre Chianca, Gallipoli is well worth the drive. Surrounded by the Ionian Sea, this town offers beaches that cater to all preferences, from bustling to secluded. The Spiaggia della Puritate is located right in the heart of this ancient town, allowing for a seamless transition from beach relaxation to exploring Gallipoli's old-world charm.
