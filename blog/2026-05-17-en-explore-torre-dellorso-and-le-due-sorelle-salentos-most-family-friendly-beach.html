Explore Torre dell'Orso and Le Due Sorelle: Salento's Most Family-Friendly Beach

Uncover the stunning landscapes of Torre dell'Orso and the legendary Le Due Sorelle, a perfect family beach getaway in Salento. Experience the essence of Puglia with our insider tips.

Photo by Massimo Virgilio on Unsplash

Welcome to Torre dell'Orso

Torre dell'Orso, a picturesque seaside gem nestled along the Adriatic coast of Puglia, beckons with its golden sands and turquoise waters. This beach, a haven for families, offers the perfect blend of natural beauty and engaging activities, making it an ideal spot for a memorable family vacation.

The Legend of Le Due Sorelle

At the heart of Torre dell'Orso lies the enchanting Le Due Sorelle, two striking sea stacks that rise majestically from the azure Adriatic waters. As local legend tells it, these two rocks are the embodiment of two sisters whose love for the sea transformed them into eternal guardians of the coast. Photogenic and resonating with local myths, Le Due Sorelle are a must-see for anyone visiting Salento.

Family-Friendly Fun

Torre dell'Orso beach offers a gentle descent into crystal-clear, shallow waters, providing a safe and inviting environment for children and less experienced swimmers. Pack a picnic and settle into a sun-soaked spot, or explore the beach's array of local cafes and gelaterie, perfect for sampling Puglia's famous gelato.

Close to Torre dell'Orso, you'll find several noteworthy attractions that cater to all ages. The Grotta della Poesia, one of the most beautiful natural swimming pools in the world, is just a short drive away. It's an ideal excursion for adventurous families willing to explore the dramatic coastal scenery of Salento.
